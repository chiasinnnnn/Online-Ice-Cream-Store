Introducing: Gula Melaka Crème

Published on 15 June 2025

Say hello to your new favorite flavor: Gula Melaka Creme - a delightful blend of creamy Normandy dairy and rich, smoky palm sugar sourced directly from Kelantan. At Fleur de Lait, we've long dreamed of honoring Malaysian roots while preserving the delicate French techniques that define our creations.

What Makes It Special?

Our chefs spent months perfecting the balance between the earthy depth of gula Melaka and the silky lightness of creme fraiche. The result? A smooth, melt-in-your-mouth scoop that finishes with just the right amount of caramelized warmth.

Where Can You Try It?

Gula Melaka Creme is now available in all Fleur de Lait locations and will be featured as our star flavor at upcoming events like the Summer Ice Cream Festival. It's a limited-edition release, so be sure to get it while it lasts!

Behind the Flavor

We believe in stories behind every scoop. Our gula Melaka is harvested by small local producers in Kelantan, sun-dried and poured traditionally. That authenticity shines through in every bite.
