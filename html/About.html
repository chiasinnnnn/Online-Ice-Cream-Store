Our Story

Founded in 2015, Fleur de Lait ("Flower of Milk" in French) began as a Parisian-inspired patisserie in Sunway City. Our name embodies the delicate artistry we bring to dairy - transforming simple milk into something as exquisite and nuanced as a blooming flower. Each creation is a petal in our bouquet of flavors.

What started as a humble boutique specializing in French desserts evolved into an artisanal ice cream atelier, where we combine traditional techniques with innovative flavors. Just as a flower unfolds its beauty gradually, we patiently craft each batch to reveal its full depth of flavor.

Fun Facts

  • Each batch is churned for exactly 18 minutes for perfect texture.
  • Our signature flavor, Lavender Honey, won the 2022 International Gelato Award.
  • We source vanilla beans directly from Sarawak.
  • Our French-style ice cream brands in Malaysia have full JAKIM halal certification since 2018.
  • 65% of our dairy comes from local Malay farmers in Negeri Sembilan's dairy belt.

Our Mission

To transform the simple pleasure of ice cream into an artful experience by combining time-honored French techniques with innovative flavor alchemy. We commit to using only the purest ingredients, crafting each batch with the precision of pastry chefs and serving every scoop with Parisian elegance.

Our Vision

To be the most beloved Malaysian-French ice cream brand, where every scoop tells a story of cultural harmony. We aim to establish Fleur de Lait as a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists, with outlets across Malaysia that showcase our nation's flavors through a Parisian lens, putting Malaysian ingredients on the global gourmet map.
